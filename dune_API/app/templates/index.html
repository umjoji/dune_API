About Dune

"Dune" is a science fiction novel first written by Frank Herbert. It is set in the distant future and tells the story of Paul Atreides, a young nobleman whose family becomes embroiled in a power struggle on the desert planet of Arrakis. Dune has sequels written by Brian Herbert and Kevin J. Anderson.

The novel explores themes of politics, religion, ecology, and human survival. "Dune" has gained widespread acclaim and is considered one of the greatest science fiction works of all time.
